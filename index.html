Why play the English?

The English Opening is a chess opening that is characterized by the move 1.c4. It is named after the English players who frequently employed this opening in the 19th century.

The English Opening aims to control the center of the board and prepare for a flexible middlegame strategy.

Main Variations

  • 1.c4 e5: Symmetrical Variation
  • 1.c4 c5: Reversed Sicilian
  • 1.c4 Nf6: Anglo-Indian Defense
  • 1.c4 e6: Agincourt
